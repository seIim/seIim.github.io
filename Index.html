About me:
Hello! I am Selim Kalici, a Mathematics and Physics student currently at SUNY Oswego. I have done Research at places like the Max Planck Institute of Astrophysics in Munich, Germany and the INAF Observatory in Naples, Italy just recently. My research was in theoretical and computational astrophysics. Specifically studying emergent properties of numerical simulations of variable stars, creating model grids based on theoretical and observed constraints, and machine learning applications.
