About me:
Hello!
I am Selim Kalici. I just graduated from SUNY Oswego with majors in mathematics and physics, and a minor in astronomy. I will be joining the Yale Astronomy department as a PhD student in Fall of 2024. My research is focused in computational astrophysics, with an emphasis on modeling stellar pulsators. I have previously done work at The Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics under Dr. Earl Bellinger and at the Capodimonte Observatory in Naples, Italy under Dr. Anupam Bhardwaj. I am apart of the YAML research group.
